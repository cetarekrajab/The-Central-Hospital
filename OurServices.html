Emergency (A&E) Information

For a life-threatening emergency, call 999.

Our A&E department is located at the Main Hospital Entrance, Ground Floor, West Wing. Get Directions Here.

View Live A&E Waiting Times

When should you visit A&E? A&E is for serious, life-threatening injuries and illnesses. For minor injuries or illnesses, please consider visiting an Urgent Treatment Centre or contacting your GP first.

For Patients & Visitors

Visiting Hours

Visiting times vary by ward to ensure our patients get the rest they need. Please check the specific times for the ward you are visiting. General hours are typically 2pm - 4pm and 6pm - 8pm.

Getting Here

We are located on Central Way, Anytown, AN1 2BH. We are easily accessible via the number 12 and 45 bus routes. On-site pay-and-display parking is available, with costs starting from £2.50 per hour.

Hospital Maps

Navigating the hospital can be tricky. We recommend downloading a map of the hospital site before your visit to easily find departments, wards, and facilities.

Facilities

We have a range of facilities on-site, including a cafe in the main atrium, a convenience shop, an outpatient pharmacy, and free Wi-Fi access for all patients and visitors.

Accessibility

We are committed to being accessible to everyone. Disabled parking bays are located near all main entrances. We also offer patient transport services and wheelchair access throughout the hospital.
