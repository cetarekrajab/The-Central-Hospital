Welcome to The Central Hospital, where your health and well-being are our highest priority. Our mission is to deliver outstanding medical care with compassion and respect for every patient we serve.

A Legacy of Excellence, A Future of Innovation

Founded in 1985 by Dr. Arjun Verma, The Central Hospital has grown from a small community clinic into a leading multispecialty hospital. For decades, we have stood as a beacon of hope and healing, building a legacy of trust one patient at a time. At the heart of our hospital is our greatest asset: a dedicated family of over 2,000 medical professionals, from nationally-recognised physicians to highly skilled surgeons and compassionate nurses. By combining world-class medical innovation with unwavering compassion, we create a supportive environment where you and your loved ones feel valued, heard, and cared for.
